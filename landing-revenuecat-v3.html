The agentic era changes everything

Three structural shifts that make RevenueCat the most strategic platform to build on right now — and the role I'd play in accelerating it.

SHIFT 01

From building to directing

By 2027, an AI agent handles the entire app lifecycle — from prompt to published product. The human becomes the product director, not the implementer. RevenueCat is already the monetization layer that agents can read, write, and act on without human intervention.

From growth hacking to autonomous growth loops

Traditional A/B testing is slow and human-intensive. Autonomous growth loops don't just run experiments — they design them, spin up content variations, monitor conversion signals across channels, adjust paywall copy based on real-time churn data, and iterate without a human in the loop.
